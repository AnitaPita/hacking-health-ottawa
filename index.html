Lobes of the Cerebrum

  • The cerebrum has 5 lobes (paired):

    1. Frontal
    2. Parietal
    3. Temporal
    4. Occipital
    5. Insular (hidden; insulated)
  • The frontal and parietal lobes are separated by the central sulcus.
  • The frontal and temporal lobes are separated by the lateral (sylvian) fissure.
  • The occipital and parietal lobes are aptly separated by the parietal occipital sulcus.
